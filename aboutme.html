MY STORY

I am a Software Engineer who wants to create simple and intuitive user interfaces that elevate the online presence. I was born and raised in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia. I moved to the United States at 12, becoming a first-generation immigrant. Although I faced many struggles, including language and cultural barriers, I worked hard to create opportunities for myself which led to earning a Full-Ride Scholarship and graduating with a degree in Business Administration. I have always been interested in working with technology, which steered me into becoming a software engineer. Feel free to contact me if you want to say hello or have any questions!
